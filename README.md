# Oil-Prices-Deep-Dive
Analysing the Brent Crude Oil Index, what influences its movements?
Oil prices have demonstrated an overall volatility in the general global sphere and seem to influenced by a myriad of factors. In focus, I laid emphasis on the oil production data by OECD
Similarly, there was focus on the exchange rates relating to the US currency - the world reserve currency.
The deep dive took cointegration analysis into account as well as checking the stationarity of the data to see its fitness for modelling and prediction.
There was a clear indication that the exchange rates as well as the oil production data was in the short and long run influencing the price index that acts as the global benchmark.
